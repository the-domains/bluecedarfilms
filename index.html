Lost Days (Music Video)

Lost Days contains a short film within itself as it follows the relationship between an alcoholic and his family, an original song created by up and coming artist Henry Maybury. The song itself falls under the genre of pop/rock. The lyrics reflect the artist's own experiences as alcoholic abuse overcame his brother.
